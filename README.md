# Nike Sales Dashboard – Power BI Business Intelligence Project

##  Project Overview

The Nike Sales Dashboard is a Business Intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, product profitability, and customer purchasing behavior. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, enabling data-driven decision-making and strategic business planning.

This project demonstrates the application of data analytics, visualization, and reporting techniques to evaluate key business metrics and identify growth opportunities within a retail sales environment.

---

## Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales and revenue performance.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
- Evaluate regional sales performance.
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Support business decisions using data-driven insights.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

- Total Sales Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Product Performance
- Category-wise Sales
- Regional Sales Distribution
- Customer Contribution Analysis

## Dashboard Analysis

### 1. Sales Performance Analysis
- Evaluated overall sales trends.
- Identified periods with peak sales activity.
- Monitored revenue growth patterns.

### 2. Product Analysis
- Determined best-selling products.
- Compared category-wise sales performance.
- Analyzed product contribution to total revenue.

### 3. Customer Analysis
- Identified high-value customers.
- Analyzed purchasing behavior.
- Evaluated customer contribution to overall sales.

### 4. Regional Analysis
- Compared sales performance across regions.
- Identified high-performing and underperforming markets.
- Evaluated geographical sales distribution.

### 5. Profitability Analysis
- Measured profit generated by products and categories.
- Identified areas impacting profitability.
- Supported strategic decision-making through profit insights.
